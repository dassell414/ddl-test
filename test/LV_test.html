    Get a car insurance quote

    When getting your Nationwide car insurance quote, it will make the process a lot simpler if you have certain items of information to hand:

    • your car details including; make, model, year of manufacture and your registration number;
    • details of the vehicles legal owner and registered keeper;
    • your driving licence and details of your driving history;
    • your car insurance history including details of any no claim discount entitlement;
    • details of any losses, accidents or claims in the past 5 years;
    • additional drivers; and
    • your credit or debit card for payments.
